Commit timing

When should you commit?

‚úÖ Commit now
Finished a logical piece of work
End of day (even if WIP)
Before pulling updates
‚ùå Not ideal
After every tiny edit
After a full week of changes
Mixing unrelated changes together
üí° Analogy
Committing is like saving a checkpoint in a video game ‚Äî do it after meaningful progress, not every footstep, but definitely before a boss fight.
